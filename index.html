About Me

Chanuka Nadeeshan

Hello! I'm Chanuka, a passionate professional photographer dedicated to freezing beautiful moments in time. With years of experience behind the lens, my expertise spans across weddings, engagements, intimate portraits, joyful events, and commercial photography.

My approach is a seamless blend of candid photojournalism and elegant portraiture. I believe that every photograph should tell a unique, authentic story and evoke deep emotions. Whether it's your grand wedding day, a milestone birthday, or a casual lifestyle shoot, I strive to create visually stunning narratives that you will cherish forever.
